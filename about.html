About Ethiopian Youth Physics Network

We are dedicated to supporting Ethiopian students and young researchers in physics by providing access to quality learning resources, journals, blogs, and Olympiad materials.

Welcome!

This channel is for Ethiopian high school students interested in physics and quantum science. Our goal is to increase understanding of physics and build the largest nationwide physics community. Here, we will:

  • Break down complex physics theories
  • Prepare for national and international physics Olympiads
  • Share physics opportunities
  • Have fun and connect with like-minded individuals

Our Mission

To inspire and educate the next generation of Ethiopian physicists through curated content, research opportunities, and community engagement.

Our Vision

To become Ethiopia's leading online hub for physics education and research, connecting students, educators, and researchers nationwide.

What We Offer

  • Weekly updated physics journals and articles
  • Educational blogs covering concepts and experiments
  • Resources for physics Olympiads and competitions
  • Guidance for research projects and publications
